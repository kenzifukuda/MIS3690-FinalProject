What is this? About Me
Welcome! You've stumbled upon my new personal website. Previously, I was using Wix to create host my website, but I've decided to give web design from scratch a try. I apologize if it's not as aesthetically appealing (yet). This website is a product of a class project I have to do to pass, my own boredom, and the desire to start creating my own content. If you follow my finsta, you know I like to keep things interesting and dramatic. I complain a lot and constantly find myself in strange, uncomfortable, and unexpected situations. This place will be a little different. Here you will find a blog for my thoughts on the world, concepts that I think need be explored or questioned further, and some recommendations for music, movies, books, etc. that I think are worth looking into. In addition, I have added a page for all my professional work experiences and projects. Will I be puttng this on my resume to show to employers? We'll see how good it looks later on.
DISCLAIMER: Nothing I write in my blog posts are likely to be based in facts because researching these things in depth is too much work rignt now. But if I am using outside research, I'll be sure to provide my sources. Besides the blog, I also have a page dedicated to my random Google searches as previously seen on my finsta. AND for when/if I start recording podcast episodes, that will be uploaded here as well. Still working on a title for the podcast, and I am taking suggestions :)
I'm hoping to add other pages as well for art or business ideas or anything else I feel like putting on here, so stay tuned...
So with that, I hope you enjoy! Scroll to the bottom if you have any comments or suggestions for the page that you'd like to send in. Or just text me, because most of you probably have less awkward way of contacting me. This is more for my own entertainment than anyone else's.
