What Is This?

Welcome! My name is Kenzi, and you've stumbled upon my new personal website. Previously, I was using Wix to create and host my website, but I've decided to give web design from scratch a try. I apologize if it's not as aesthetically appealing (yet). This website is a product of a Babson class project I have to do to pass, my own boredom, and the desire to start creating my own content.

Here you will find a blog for my thoughts on the world, concepts that I think need be explored or questioned further, and some recommendations for music, movies, books, etc. that I think are worth looking into. In addition, I have added a page for all my professional work experiences and projects. Will I be puttng this on my resume to show to employers? We'll see how good it looks later on.

DISCLAIMER: A lot of what I'm writing here is opinion, but if I am using outside research, I'll be sure to provide my sources. Besides the blog, I also have a page dedicated to my random Google searches because these are the thoughts that keep me up at night and I need you to know that.

I'm hoping to add other pages as well for art or business ideas or anything else I feel like putting on here, so stay tuned...

I hope you enjoy! And just know, this is more for my own entertainment than anyone else's.
