Kenzi's Project Proposal

What is your website about?

I want to create a personal website similar to one I created over the summer. It would serve as a personal blog, and a place to house some of my professional experiences and personal interests.

What is the objective of your website?

I will use this website as additional material for potential employers to learn more about me and my experiences. It is also a way for me to start blogging again. Which is something that I stopped doing after school started again.

Who is your target audience?

The professional experience aspect of my website would highlight some of the projects and work I have done over the last few years. It is targeted towards potential employers to use as further reference material when considering me for positions within their company.

The personal blog and my other interests would be targeted towards myself, friends, and family. It would be used as a way to update people on my life, and discuss topics that I find interesting and worth researching further.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

The personal blog page will have posts I hope to update weekly. It would be used as an external diary for others to learn more about me and what I am up to on a weekly basis.

I would also have a page just for my resume for employers to reference.

Along with my resume, I would include a page that displays some of the projects listed on my resume, so employers could see and experience the projects I have worked on.

I also want to include a page that focuses on new ideas I have for businesses or projects I may potentially want to start. I am currently trying to start a podcast, so that may be something I want to add as an additional page.

I would include an about me page as well, and a page to contact me,

The landing page would have links to all the other pages, and include pictures and design that speaks to my personality and identity.
